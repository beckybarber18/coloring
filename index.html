INSTRUCTIONS

Cover as much of the arena in your color as possible.
Player 1 (left) controls the ball using the WASD keys.
Player 2 (right) controls the ball using the arrow keys.

Watch out for power ups!
Green bombs cover the surrounding tiles in your color.
Blue ice cubes freeze your opponent for a few seconds.
Red crosses cover a horizontal and vertical line of tiles in your color.

Start the game by choosing your color palette above!
